# 📊 ESG & Financial Performance Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project contains two interactive Tableau dashboards built using an ESG & Financial Performance dataset. The dashboards provide insights into company financial performance, ESG scores, sustainability metrics, and industry-wise comparisons.

# 📁 Dataset Information

| Column | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| CompanyID | Unique company identifier |
| CompanyName | Company name |
| Industry | Industry sector |
| Region | Company region |
| Year | Reporting year |
| Revenue | Annual revenue (Million USD) |
| ProfitMargin | Net profit margin (%) |
| MarketCap | Market capitalization (Million USD) |
| GrowthRate | Year-over-year revenue growth (%) |
| ESG_Overall | Overall ESG Score (0–100) |
| ESG_Environmental | Environmental Score |
| ESG_Social | Social Responsibility Score |
| ESG_Governance | Governance Score |
| CarbonEmissions | Annual Carbon Emissions (Tons CO₂) |
| WaterUsage | Annual Water Usage (m³) |
| EnergyConsumption | Annual Energy Consumption (MWh) |

# 🎛 Interactive Filters

The following filters are available and **work across both dashboards**:

- 🌍 Region
- 📅 Year
- 🏭 Industry

These filters allow users to interactively explore financial and sustainability performance across different regions, industries, and reporting years.

---

# 💡 Key Insights

### Executive Financial Dashboard

- Technology industry generated the highest revenue.
- Europe recorded the highest regional revenue.
- Revenue showed a consistent upward trend over time.
- Profit margin remained relatively stable across the reporting years.
- Technology industry also had the highest market capitalization.

### ESG Dashboard

- Finance industry achieved the highest average ESG score.
- Energy industry recorded the highest carbon emissions, water usage, and energy consumption.
- Europe showed the highest average ESG performance among regions.
- Carbon emissions and energy consumption increased steadily over the reporting period.
- ESG performance varied significantly across industries.
